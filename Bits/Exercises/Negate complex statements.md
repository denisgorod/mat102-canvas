Negate each of the following logical statements:

1. $\forall x,\, [P(x) \Longrightarrow Q(x)]$

2. $\exists x,\, [P(x) \Longleftrightarrow Q(x)]$

3. $\exists x,\, [(P(x) \wedge Q(x)) \Longrightarrow R(x)]$

4. $\forall x,\, [P(x) \Longrightarrow (Q(x) \Longrightarrow R(x))]$

>[!question] Exercise
> Write the negation of each statement. Your answer should be a logically equivalent statement that does not begin with "it is not the case that."
