Define a relation on $\mathbb{Z}$ by $a\,R\,b$ if there is an $n \in \mathbb{Z}$ such that $b = na$; that is, $a$ divides $b$.

>[!question] Exercise
> Determine whether $R$ is reflexive, symmetric, anti-symmetric, transitive, or total.
