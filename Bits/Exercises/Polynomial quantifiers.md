Let $P$ be the set of all polynomials.

1. $\forall x \in \mathbb{R},\, \forall p \in P,\, p(x) = 0$

2. $\forall x \in \mathbb{R},\, \exists p \in P,\, p(x) = 0$

3. $\exists x \in \mathbb{R},\, \forall p \in P,\, p(x) = 0$

4. $\exists p \in P,\, \forall x \in \mathbb{R},\, p(x) = 0$

5. $\forall p \in P,\, \exists x \in \mathbb{R},\, p(x) = 0$

6. $\exists x \in \mathbb{R},\, \exists p \in P,\, p(x) = 0$

>[!question] Exercise
> Determine whether each of the above statements is true or false. If true, prove it. If false, give a counterexample.
