Determine whether the following two statements are logically equivalent:

$$[\forall x,\, P(x)] \Longrightarrow [\forall x,\, Q(x)] \qquad \text{and} \qquad \forall x,\, [P(x) \Longrightarrow Q(x)].$$

>[!question] Exercise
> Are these equivalent? If yes, explain why. If no, find a concrete example (specific meanings for $P$ and $Q$) where one is true and the other is false.
