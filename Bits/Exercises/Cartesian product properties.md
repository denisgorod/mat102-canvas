Let $A = \{\clubsuit, \heartsuit\}$ and $B = \{\uparrow, \downarrow\}$.

>[!question] Exercise
> 1. What is $A \times \emptyset$?
> 2. Is $A \times B = B \times A$?
> 3. Is $(A \times B) \times A = A \times (B \times A)$?
