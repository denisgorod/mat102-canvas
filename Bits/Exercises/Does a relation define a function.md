Recall that a relation on sets $A$ and $B$ is a subset of $A \times B$, and it is:
- **left-total** if every element of $A$ appears in at least one pair
- **functional** if every element of $A$ appears in at most one pair

A function requires both properties.

Suppose $A$ and $B$ are sets with $A \subseteq B$. Define a relation by $S_g = A \times A \subseteq A \times B$.

>[!question] Exercise
> Does $S_g$ define a function from $A$ to $B$? Explain why or why not, checking both left-totality and functionality.
