The preimage of a set $V \subseteq B$ under $f: A \rightarrow B$ is
$$f^{-1}(V) = \{x \in A : f(x) \in V\}.$$

>[!question] Exercise
> Let $f: \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ be given by $f(x) = x^2$. Find $f^{-1}((-1,1))$.
