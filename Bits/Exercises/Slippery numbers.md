Definitions can be built by combining conditions. Here's one that uses the divisibility notion you just practiced.

>[!d] Definition
> A positive integer $b$ is **slippery** if $3 \mid b$ but $2 \nmid b$.

>[!question] Exercise
> Write out the first five slippery numbers.
