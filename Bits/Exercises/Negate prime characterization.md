Let $\mathbb{P}$ denote the set of prime numbers. The following is a correct statement:

$$\forall p \in \mathbb{Z}^+,\, \bigl[p \in \mathbb{P} \Longleftrightarrow \bigl(\forall a \in \mathbb{Z},\, \forall b \in \mathbb{Z},\, (\exists k \in \mathbb{Z},\, pk = ab) \Longrightarrow \exists \ell \in \mathbb{Z}\, [p\ell = a \vee p\ell = b]\bigr)\bigr].$$

>[!question] Exercise
> Write the negation of this sentence.
