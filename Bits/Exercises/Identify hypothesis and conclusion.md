Being able to separate the hypothesis from the conclusion is a fundamental skill. Practice it here.

>[!question] Exercise
> For each of the following (true) mathematical results, identify the hypothesis and conclusion.
> - If $x$ is a positive real number, then there is a positive real number $y$ such that $y^2 = x$.
> - There is a unique integer $e$ such that for all integers $n$, $e \cdot n = n$.
> - There are infinitely many prime numbers.
> - For any positive real number $\epsilon > 0$, there is a positive integer $n$ such that $1/n < \epsilon$.
