Not every sentence is a proposition. Practice telling them apart.

>[!question] Exercise
> Identify whether each sentence below is a proposition, a predicate, or neither.
> 1. Today is Friday.
> 2. What time is it?
> 3. The moon is made out of cheese.
> 4. $x^2 > \pi$.
> 5. There is an integer solution to the equation $2x^2 - 3y^3 = 4$.
> 6. $x^2 + y^2 = 1$.
> 7. If there is still food leftover, I'll have a second helping.
