Prove the forward direction of Proposition 5: if $f: S \to T$ is invertible, then $f$ is bijective.

That is: assuming there exists a function $g: T \to S$ such that $f \circ g = \mathrm{id}_T$ and $g \circ f = \mathrm{id}_S$, show that $f$ is both injective and surjective.

>[!question] Exercise
>Write out a complete proof. Your proof should clearly establish injectivity and surjectivity separately.
