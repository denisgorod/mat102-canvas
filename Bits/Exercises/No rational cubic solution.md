>[!question] Exercise
> Show that $x^3 + x^2 = 1$ has no rational solution. *Hint*: proceed by contradiction, setting $x = a/b$ for some $a \in \mathbb{Z}$ and $b \in \mathbb{Z}^+$. Try arguing about the parity of $a$ and $b$.
