Two functions are equal if and only if they have the same domain, the same codomain, and the same rule. All three pieces of data matter.

>[!question] Exercise
> Which, if any, of the following functions are equal to one another? Justify your answer.
> 1. $f:\{0,1\} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}, \quad x \mapsto x$
> 2. $g:\{0,1\} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}, \quad x \mapsto x^{2}$
> 3. $h:\{0,1\} \rightarrow \{0,1\}, \quad x \mapsto x$
