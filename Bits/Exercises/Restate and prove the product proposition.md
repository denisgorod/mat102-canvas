The proof of "if $a$ and $b$ are both even, then $ab$ is even" revealed something interesting: we only ever used the fact that *one* of the factors was even. The hypothesis was stronger than necessary.

>[!question] Exercise
> Restate the proposition with a weaker hypothesis that accounts for the fact that only one factor needs to be even. Prove your restated proposition.
