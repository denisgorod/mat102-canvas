>[!question] Exercise
> Prove each of the following by contradiction:
> 1. If $x$ and $y$ are real numbers whose sum $x + y$ is irrational, then at least one of $x$ or $y$ must be irrational.
> 2. If $p$ is a prime number greater than 2, then $p$ is odd.
> 3. If $p$ is a prime number, then $\sqrt{p}$ is irrational.
