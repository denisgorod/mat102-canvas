Here is a new definition for you to work with.

>[!d] Definition
> Let $a$ and $b$ be positive whole numbers. We say that $a$ **divides** $b$ if there exists another positive whole number $n$ such that $b = na$. We write this as $a \mid b$.

>[!question] Exercise
> Give three examples of pairs $(a, b)$ such that $a \mid b$. Give three examples of pairs $(a, b)$ such that $a \nmid b$.
