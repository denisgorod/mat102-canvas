You've seen that the contrapositive proves "if $n^2$ is even then $n$ is even."

>[!question] Exercise
> Let $n \in \mathbb{Z}^*$. Show that $n$ is even if and only if $n^2$ is even. (You need to prove both directions.) Conclude that $n$ is odd if and only if $n^2$ is odd.
