Let $S$ be the set of all students in a classroom, and let $B(a, b)$ be the statement "student $a$ has the same birthday as student $b$."

>[!question] Exercise
> Write each of the following mathematical statements in plain language, avoiding variables if possible:
> - $\forall a \in S,\, \forall b \in S,\, B(a,b)$
> - $\forall a \in S,\, \exists b \in S,\, B(a,b)$
> - $\exists a \in S,\, \forall b \in S,\, B(a,b)$
> - $\exists a \in S,\, \exists b \in S,\, B(a,b)$
