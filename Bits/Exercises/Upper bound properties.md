Consider $\mathbb{Z}^+$ with the divisibility relation $a \preceq b$ iff $a \mid b$, and the subset $S = \{8, 12, 36\}$.

>[!question] Exercise
> 1. Prove that 72 is the least upper bound of $S$ (that is, 72 is an upper bound, and no smaller positive integer is also an upper bound).
> 2. Prove that if $n$ is an upper bound of $S$, then $nk$ is an upper bound of $S$ for any $k \in \mathbb{Z}^+$.
