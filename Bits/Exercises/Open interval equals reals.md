Show that $|(0, 1)| = |\mathbb{R}|$ by constructing an explicit bijection between the open interval $(0, 1)$ and the set of all real numbers $\mathbb{R}$.

You may use standard facts about functions like the tangent function, if helpful.

>[!question] Exercise
>Provide an explicit formula for your bijection and verify that it is both injective and surjective.
